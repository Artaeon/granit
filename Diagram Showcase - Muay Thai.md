## Clinch Strategy

```diagram
type: tree
title: Clinch Positions
Inside clinch (double collar tie)
  > Opponent pushes: Drag down > Knee
  > Opponent pulls: Off-balance > Sweep
  > Opponent passive: Turn > Knee to body
  > Opponent breaks: Elbow > Re-clinch
```

## Fight Strategy

```diagram
type: timeline
title: 5 Round Fight Plan
Round 1 Teep and jab, establish range and rhythm
Round 2 Low kicks to lead leg, test the clinch
Round 3 Body knees in clinch, elbows when close
Round 4 Sweeps and dumps, accumulate damage
Round 5 All out, go for the finish
```
