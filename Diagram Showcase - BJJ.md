## Guard Decisions

```diagram
type: tree
title: Closed Guard (Top) Options
You are in Closed Guard
  > Opponent collar grip: Posture up > Break grip > Stand to pass
  > Opponent passive: Hand on hip > Stand up > Toreando pass
  > Opponent pulls high guard: Stack pass > Drive forward > Half guard
  > Opponent opens guard: Knee slice > Side control
```

## Escape Strategy

```diagram
type: tree
title: Side Control Escapes
Trapped in Side Control
  > Frame on face: Shrimp out > Reguard > Closed guard
  > Get underhook: Come to knees > Single leg takedown
  > Bridge and roll: Reversal > End in mount
  > Opponent goes high: Ghost escape > Take the back
```

## Technique Comparison

```diagram

| Knee Slice | Medium | High | Low | Half Guard |
| Stack Pass | Slow | Very High | Low | Closed Guard |
| Leg Drag | Fast | Medium | Medium | De La Riva |
```
