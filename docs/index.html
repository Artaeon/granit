Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Notes, tasks, goals, habits, calendar, AI, and daily planning. One binary. One keyboard. Zero subscriptions.

✨

12 AI Bots

Auto-tagger, auto-linker, summarizer, flashcards, tone adjuster, writing coach, and more. Runs locally on qwen2.5:0.5b. Auto-pulls model on first launch.

⌨️

Full Vim Mode

Modal editing with text objects, marks, macros, search highlighting, dot repeat, and ex commands. Everything you expect from Vim.

🎨

35 Themes

Catppuccin, Tokyo Night, Gruvbox, Nord, Dracula, and 30 more. Plus a custom theme editor to create your own palette.

✅

Task Manager

7 views including Kanban and Eisenhower Matrix. Subtasks, dependencies, snooze, templates, recurring auto-creation, and 10-deep undo.

🎯

Goal Manager

Track long-term goals with milestones, due dates, recurring reviews, progress sparklines, theme colors, and task linking.

🔍

Search Everything

Ctrl+/ opens fuzzy search across notes, tasks, goals, and habits. Ranked results with instant navigation to any item.

📅

Daily Planner

Time-blocked schedule with goal integration. Copy plan to clipboard or export as markdown for sharing with your team.

🔀

Git & Nextcloud Sync

Built-in git sync with auto-commit and conflict resolution. Nextcloud WebDAV bidirectional sync. Your data, your servers.

🔗

Obsidian Compatible

Full support for [[wikilinks]], YAML frontmatter, backlinks, and the same folder structure. Use your existing vault as-is.

🧩

Plugin System

Extend with scripts and Lua plugins. 16 core plugins included. Hook into save, open, create, and delete events.

🔒

Encryption

AES-256-GCM encryption for sensitive notes. Secure your vault for safe syncing across devices without compromising privacy.

📐

8 Layouts

Default, Writer, Minimal, Reading, Dashboard, Zen, Taskboard, and Research modes. Adapt the UI to your workflow.

Showcase

Built for how you work.

A deeper look at what makes Granit different.

A real editor, in your terminal.

Syntax-highlighted Markdown with multi-cursor, wikilink autocomplete, Ghost Writer AI, and rendered view mode with Mermaid diagrams.

  • Multi-cursor editing with Ctrl+D
  • Ghost Writer: inline AI suggestions (Tab to accept)
  • 18 built-in snippets and bracket auto-close
  • Rendered view mode with callouts and diagrams

Tasks, goals, and plans. Unified.

7 task views, goal manager with milestones and reviews, daily planner with clipboard export, and Eisenhower Matrix.

  • Subtasks, dependencies, snooze, and templates
  • Recurring tasks auto-create on completion
  • Goal-to-task linking with progress tracking
  • Copy daily plan to clipboard for your team
  • Search Everything with Ctrl+/
