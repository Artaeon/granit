# Welcome to Granit

Granit is a terminal-based knowledge manager that is fully compatible with your Obsidian vault.

## Getting Started

## Philosophy

Granit believes in:
- **Plain text** — Your notes are just Markdown files
- **Speed** — Everything should feel instant
- **Compatibility** — Works with your existing Obsidian vault
